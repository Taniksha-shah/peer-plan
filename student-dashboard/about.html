What is PeerPlan?

PeerPlan is a productivity platform designed specifically for students who want to stay organized, focused, and connected. We understand that student life can get overwhelming — with classes, assignments, exams, group projects, and personal goals. PeerPlan brings everything together in one simple, effective dashboard.

Why We Built PeerPlan?

Because students deserve more than scattered to-do lists and constant distractions.
We built PeerPlan to help you:
Plan your entire month with clarity. Stay focused using the Pomodoro technique. Collaborate with peers through scheduled study group video calls. Whether you're preparing for exams, managing your workload, or trying to build better habits, PeerPlan helps you stay on track, one day at a time.

What Makes PeerPlan Different?

Designed for students, by students. Combines planning, focus tools, and collaboration in one place. Clean, distraction-free interface that encourages productivity. Our Vision - We’re not just building a planner. We’re building a student workspace that grows with you — through semesters, projects, and late-night study sessions. Because when students support each other, everyone moves forward.
